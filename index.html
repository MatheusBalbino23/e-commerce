Our History

It all started with a shared passion for exceptional coffee. In 2010, a group of friends, united by their love for grain, decided to turn this passion into an enterprise. With the vision of offering coffee lovers a unique and unforgettable experience, our e-commerce was born.

From the beginning, our goal was clear: to bring the best coffee, from the best origins, straight to your cup. We travel the world, exploring plantations and meeting dedicated farmers who share our passion for quality. Each selected grain is the result of hard work and a commitment to excellence.

Over the years, our commitment to quality and sustainability has guided us. We work directly with small producers, ensuring fair and sustainable practices that respect both the environment and local communities. This direct relationship allows us to offer coffees that are not only delicious, but also ethically produced.

Today, we are proud to offer a wide selection of specialty coffees that suit all palates. Whether you love a full-bodied espresso or a smooth filtered coffee, we have the perfect blend for you. Every product on our website is rigorously tested and approved by our team of experts, ensuring only the best reaches you.

We are constantly innovating and expanding our horizons, but one thing remains the same: our commitment to quality and to you, our customer. We thank you for being part of our journey and allowing us to share our passion for coffee.
